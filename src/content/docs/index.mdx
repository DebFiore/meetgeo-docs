**MeetGEO is the autonomous Generative Engine Optimization platform.** We help brands get cited by AI engines — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews — by deploying the technical infrastructure those engines actually parse from your HTML, then monitoring weekly to show you exactly which queries cite your brand and which cite your competitors.

## The gap is real

When a brand first connects to MeetGEO, their AI Visibility Score almost always lands in the **Critical band (0–20)** — meaning AI engines aren't citing them at all in category-relevant queries. That's the starting line for most brands, regardless of how well they rank in classical SEO. AI citation is a different problem with different solutions, and most brands haven't had anyone do this work specifically yet. The fixes that close the gap are technical, measurable, and verifiable.

## What MeetGEO does for you

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Deploy structured data schema" icon="puzzle">
    Generates and deploys a JSON-LD entity graph (Organization + WebSite + page-level entities, all cross-referenced by `@id`) to every page on your site. This is the single biggest lever for AI citation — verify it any time in your MeetGEO Schema Coverage report.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Add FAQPage schema" icon="approve-check">
    Detects FAQ-shaped content on your site and wraps it in `FAQPage` JSON-LD. AI engines disproportionately cite FAQ-marked content because it's pre-structured into quotable Q&A pairs. Highest-ROI single addition.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Monitor weekly across 5 LLMs" icon="magnifier">
    Every Monday, sends 10 category-relevant queries to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Tracks brand mentions, competitor mentions, sentiment, and which brands the LLMs recommend first.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Surface actionable next steps" icon="rocket">
    The Weekly Insights card converts monitoring data into 2–4 prioritized recommendations — content depth fixes, identity gaps, page-expansion outlines — that you can either delegate to MeetGEO or do yourself.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

We also generate and deploy an `llms.txt` file for your site. It's a proposed standard with low adoption among AI crawlers today, but the deployment cost is near-zero and it's reasonable future-proofing. **It's not where citation impact comes from** — see the [llms.txt page](/concepts/llmstxt/) for where it fits in the priority order.
