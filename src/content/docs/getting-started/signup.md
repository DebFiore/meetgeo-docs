## What you get during the trial

7 days of full product access:

- Connect any of WordPress, Shopify, Vercel, or Cloudflare
- Auto-deploy JSON-LD entity-graph schema to every page on your site (the biggest citation lever)
- Auto-detect FAQ-shaped content and wrap it in `FAQPage` schema
- Auto-deploy `llms.txt` (low-impact future-proofing — see [where it fits](/concepts/llmstxt/))
- Run your first weekly Monitor scan across all 5 LLMs
- See your AI Visibility Score
- See Top Competitors with sentiment + position-weighted ranking
- Get Weekly Insights with prioritized recommendations

No credit card required to start.

## What happens on day 7

The dashboard locks and shows a Subscribe button. Clicking it opens Stripe Checkout where you can pick a plan.

Until you subscribe:

- Schemas + `llms.txt` files already deployed to your site **stay in place** — we don't tear them down on trial expiry
- Future weekly monitor runs pause until your subscription is active
- All historical data on your dashboard remains accessible once you subscribe

For pricing details, see [meetgeo.ai/pricing](https://meetgeo.ai/pricing) or email hello@meetgeo.ai.

## What you do in your first session

1. **Sign up** at [meetgeo.ai/signup](https://meetgeo.ai/signup) with your email and a password.
2. **Confirm your email** via the link sent to your inbox.
3. **Add your first site** — domain, brand name, brand description.
4. **Connect your platform** — see [Connect your platform](/getting-started/connect-platform/).
5. **Wait ~5 minutes** for the first auto-schema generation to complete and deploy.
6. **Optionally fill the Guide Your llms.txt form** to customize your `llms.txt` content.
7. **Wait until next Monday 03:00 UTC** for your first full LLM monitoring run.

The first weekly scan is when the dashboard becomes interesting — that's where the AI Visibility Score, Top Competitors, and Weekly Insights start populating with real data.

## What to look at first

Before your first weekly scan completes, the dashboard surfaces are mostly empty. Two cards become useful immediately:

- **Pages to Expand** — populates as soon as your first schema generation completes (within minutes of platform connect). Shows pages on your site that don't yet have enough depth to be quotable by AI engines.
- **Latest llms.txt** — also populates immediately. Shows what MeetGEO generated and deployed. Click "Guide Your llms.txt" to customize.

After your first weekly scan (Monday 03:00 UTC), the rest of the dashboard fills in.
